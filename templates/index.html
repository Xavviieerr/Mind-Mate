Hello there!

Mind mate is a reliable chatbot that is here to give you the support of a personal tutor, help you revise and help you become aware of your learning process and understand how you learn in general. Get started with 3 simple steps
  • Upload a pdf document.
  • Click on the "Generate questions" button to get questions.
  • And provide answers to the questions the model asks you.
After answering all the questions the model give ways to improve your understanding of the pdf you gave while improving your ability of underatanding of your thought process.
